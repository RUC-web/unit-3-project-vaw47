Swiftly and with Style

History

Our company got its start in 1843 by French immigrant, Roger Alfonz. Our slogan stems from that time, when refridgeration was in its infancy and the dead had to be buried with dispatch, particularly in summer. Since then, we've been at the forefront of the funeral industry. In 1974 Ernest Alfonz launched our unique, all-inclusive service, taking responsibility for all aspects and arrangements of the funeral. He believed that he could best help grieving families by allowing them to outsource all funereal arrangements to him, participating in the process as much, or as little, as they chose. This is the principle by which we still operate; it is our job to make the process of burial as easy and stress-free as possible for our clients.

Today

We were among the first funeral companies to incorporate the internet into our service, but we still maintain the personal touch. As a small, family owned company, we are able to look over every service order with care. Clients are then contacted by a real person to clarify and refine their instructions so that we can give the best possible service for you and your loved one.
